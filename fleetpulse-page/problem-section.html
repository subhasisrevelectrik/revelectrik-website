The Problem

Why Fleet Managers Are Flying Blind

Legacy telematics weren't built for electric drivetrains. CAN bus data stays trapped on the vehicle — until it's too late.

Problem 01

Blind Spots Kill Uptime

Fleet managers discover failures after vehicles are stranded. Without real-time CAN-level visibility, maintenance is always reactive and expensive.

Problem 02

OBD-II Isn't Enough

Generic scanners read a fraction of available CAN signals. Proprietary BMS data, motor controller thermals, and coolant diagnostics stay invisible.

Problem 03

Vendor Lock-In Traps

Telematics providers own your fleet data, charge per vehicle per month, and make switching painful. Your operational intelligence shouldn't be held hostage.
