The Problem

Why Fleet Managers Are Flying Blind

Legacy telematics weren't built for electric drivetrains. CAN bus data stays trapped on the vehicle — until it's too late.

Problem 01

Blind Spots Kill Uptime

Fleet managers discover failures after vehicles are stranded. Without real-time CAN-level visibility, maintenance is always reactive and expensive.

Problem 02

OBD-II Isn't Enough

Generic scanners read a fraction of available CAN signals. Proprietary BMS data, motor controller thermals, and coolant diagnostics stay invisible.

Problem 03

Vendor Lock-In Traps

Telematics providers own your fleet data, charge per vehicle per month, and make switching painful. Your operational intelligence shouldn't be held hostage.

How It Works

From CAN Bus to Dashboard in 4 Steps

No proprietary hardware. No vendor lock-in. Full data ownership from day one.

1
Step 01

Install

LILYGO T-SIM7600G-H ESP32-WROVER edge device connects via OBD-II port or direct CAN tap. Under 1 hour, no vehicle downtime.

2
Step 02

Capture

CAN frames decoded via DBC files, batched to Apache Parquet, buffered offline with automatic retry.

3
Step 03

Stream

Compressed Parquet uploads to your AWS S3 bucket. Lambda auto-decodes on arrival, no manual pipeline.

4
Step 04

Visualize

React dashboard. Any vehicle, any signal, any time range. Zoom, pan, and export with one click.

Capabilities

Everything Your Fleet Intelligence Needs

Built for real CAN bus complexity, not watered-down OBD-II readings.

DBC-Driven Decoding

Supports J1939, UDS, and OEM proprietary DBC files. Decode any signal your vehicle exposes on the CAN bus.

Interactive Dashboard

Plotly.js-powered time-series charts. Zoom, pan, and export any signal. Multi-vehicle comparison in one view.

Edge Buffering

Local Parquet storage survives connectivity gaps. Automatic retry queues ensure zero data loss, even offline.

Your Data, Your Cloud

Deploys into your own AWS account. S3, Lambda, Athena — you own the infrastructure and every byte of data.

LTTB Downsampling

Largest-Triangle-Three-Buckets algorithm renders 100K+ data points smoothly. Perceptually lossless visualization.

Infrastructure as Code

AWS CDK stack. One command deploys the entire platform: S3, Lambda, Athena, API Gateway, Cognito.
